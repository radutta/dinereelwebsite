Our Story

RD, AC, and SS, passionate foodies and tech enthusiasts, joined forces to create a game-changing video-based food menu app. Their shared vision was to enhance the dining experience by showcasing mouthwatering visuals of dishes. With RD's coding expertise, AC's design skills, and SS's business acumen, they embarked on their entrepreneurial journey. Tirelessly working day and night, they developed an app named "Dinereel." It featured captivating videos of delectable dishes, allowing users to explore, customize, and order with ease. As word spread, Dinereel gained traction, attracting food enthusiasts and restaurants alike, transforming the way people discovered and ordered their favorite meals.
